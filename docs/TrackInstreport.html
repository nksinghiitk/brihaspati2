Tracking Reports

In this page there are three links.

    1. User Reports

   Under this link Instructor can track reports by selecting user name. it provide the all detail of particular user(like user course,usergroup,userrole and user joining date etc).

  2.. Course/Lesson Reports

   Instructor can track reports of Course by selecting the user. it give the all details of particular course which join by user. it give the information about the related topics,time,publish and unpublish file.

  3.. Test/quiz Reports

   Instructor can track the report of any user's Quiz detail. by this instructor get the information about the quiz that particular user complete it or not attempt and also other detail related to Quiz.
